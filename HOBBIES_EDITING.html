    Favourite Game

I rarely game, but if I do, it's definitely Among Us. Among Us is a murder mystery game
that follows a group of Crewmates who have imposters among them. The imposter's goal
is to Sabotoge their ship and kill everyone. The Crewmates need to complete tasks and
vote out the Imposter.






Why do I enjoy playing Among Us?

         Playing as an imposter is exhilarating for me. The game involves deduction, deception, and strategy as players

         try to figure out who the imposters are or how to successfully carry out imposter missions without getting caught.
